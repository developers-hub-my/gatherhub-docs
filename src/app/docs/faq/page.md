## General Questions

### What is GatherHub?

GatherHub is an event management platform that helps organizations manage events from registration and payment through to attendance tracking and certificate generation.

### Who can use GatherHub?

GatherHub is designed for event organizers in Malaysia who run trainings, workshops, seminars, conferences, and other gatherings that require registration and attendance tracking.

### Is GatherHub free?

GatherHub has no monthly subscription fee. For paid events, there's a transaction fee of 3% + RM 1 per successful payment. Free events have no fees.

## Registration Questions

### How do I register for an event?

1. Visit the event page
2. Select your ticket type
3. Fill in your details
4. Complete payment (if applicable)
5. Receive confirmation email

### Can I register multiple people at once?

Yes, most events allow you to purchase multiple tickets in one order. You'll be asked to provide details for each attendee.

### How do I cancel my registration?

If the event allows cancellations:
1. Log in to your participant portal
2. Find your registration
3. Click Cancel Registration
4. Follow refund instructions

Check the event's refund policy for details.

### Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

This depends on the event settings. Contact the event organizer to request a transfer if needed.

## Payment Questions

### What payment methods are accepted?

GatherHub supports:
- FPX (online banking)
- DuitNow QR
- Manual bank transfer

### Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels. GatherHub is PCI DSS compliant and never stores your card details.

### When will I be charged?

For online payments (FPX, DuitNow), you're charged immediately upon completion. For manual transfers, you initiate the transfer yourself.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund availability depends on the event's refund policy. Check the event page or contact the organizer.

### Why did my payment fail?

Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Bank security blocks
- Session timeout
- Technical issues

Try again or use a different payment method.

## Check-In Questions

### How do I check in to an event?

Show your QR code (from email or portal) to the check-in staff. They'll scan it to record your attendance.

### What if I forgot my ticket?

Staff can look you up by name or email. Have your confirmation email or ID ready.

### Can I check in to just some sessions?

If the event has session-level check-in, yes. You'll check in separately for each session you attend.

## Certificate Questions

### How do I get my certificate?

If you meet the attendance requirements:
1. The organizer generates certificates after the event
2. You receive an email notification
3. Download from your participant portal

### Why don't I have a certificate?

Possible reasons:
- Event hasn't ended
- Certificates not yet generated
- You didn't meet attendance requirements
- Contact organizer for clarification

### Can I verify my certificate?

Yes. Every certificate has a QR code and number that can be verified at gatherhub.app/verify.

### Can I get a replacement certificate?

Contact the event organizer. They can regenerate your certificate if needed.

## Account Questions

### How do I create an account?

You can:
1. Register for an event (account created automatically)
2. Sign up at gatherhub.app

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Follow the reset link

### How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Note that this is permanent.

## Organizer Questions

### How do I create an event?

1. Log in to GatherHub
2. Go to Events
3. Click Create Event
4. Follow the creation wizard

### How much does it cost to organize events?

Free events have no cost. For paid events, GatherHub charges 3% + RM 1 per successful transaction.

### Can I have multiple organizers?

Yes. Add crew members with the Organizer role to share full management access.

### How do I export my data?

Go to Participants or Reports, then click Export. Choose CSV or Excel format.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

GatherHub works best on:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

Use the latest version for best experience.

### Is there a mobile app?

GatherHub is a web application optimized for mobile browsers. No separate app is needed.

### Why is the page loading slowly?

Try:
- Clearing browser cache
- Using a different browser
- Checking internet connection
- Disabling browser extensions

## Privacy Questions

### How is my data used?

Your data is used to:
- Process registrations
- Send event communications
- Generate certificates
- Provide event analytics to organizers

### Who can see my information?

- Other participants cannot see your information

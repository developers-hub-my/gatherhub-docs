GatherHub is the operational hub for events and activities. Whether you're running trainings, workshops, seminars, or conferences, GatherHub helps you manage the entire event lifecycle without spreadsheets or manual tracking.

---

## What you can do with GatherHub

### For Event Organizers

- **Create events** with custom registration forms, multiple ticket types, and flexible pricing
- **Manage participants** through a centralized dashboard with search, filters, and bulk actions
- **Process payments** via FPX, DuitNow QR, or manual bank transfer
- **Track attendance** with QR code check-in at event, session, and activity levels
- **Generate certificates** automatically based on attendance requirements
- **Communicate** with participants through targeted email campaigns

### For Crew Members

- **Collaborate** with role-based access for coordinators, staff, volunteers, and speakers
- **Run check-in** stations with the mobile-friendly QR scanner
- **Monitor attendance** in real-time through the dashboard

### For Participants

- **Register** for events and select sessions or activities
- **Receive** tickets with QR codes for seamless check-in
- **Access** your personal portal to view registrations and download certificates

## Platform Overview

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Event Management | Create and configure events with sessions, activities, and custom fields |
| Ticketing | Multiple ticket types with pricing, capacity limits, and discount codes |
| Registration | Custom forms with validation and bulk import support |
| Payments | FPX, DuitNow QR (BayarCash), and manual bank transfer |
| Check-In | QR scanning with multi-level attendance tracking |
| Certificates | Customizable templates with eligibility rules and QR verification |
| Communication | Blast email campaigns with templates and tracking |
| Reports | Attendance, revenue, and participant data exports |
